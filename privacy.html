Moonveil - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how your privacy is protected when using the Moonveil Discord Bot.

1. Information Collected

  • Server ID, Channel ID, and User ID for bot configuration
  • Command usage for logs and cooldown management
  • Economy and game-related data (e.g., money, characters, stats)

2. How We Use Data

We use your data solely to provide the intended bot features. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

3. Data Security

All data is securely stored using MongoDB and is accessible only to the developer. We take all reasonable precautions to prevent data leaks or misuse.

4. Data Deletion

You may request data deletion by removing the bot from your server. For complete removal, contact us directly via Discord.

5. Contact

For questions, concerns, or data access/removal requests, please visit: https://gaeuly.my.id/
